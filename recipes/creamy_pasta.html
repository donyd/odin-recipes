Creamy Pasta

Description

This quick pasta dish is easy to make

This flavourful and protein rich recipe can be adjusted to suit spiciness while still being rich and sumptuous.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Garlic Cloves
  • Vegan Cream
  • Mustard Powder
  • Pepper
  • Vegan Salmon
  • Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Bring some water to a boil and add the pasta
  2. Cook al dente
  3. Chop up the garlic finely
  4. Heat some olive oil in a pan and add the garlic
  5. Allow to saute till the fragrance releases
